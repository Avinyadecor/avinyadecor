About Us

Incorporated in 2024, Avinya is a pioneer in hostel and PG furniture manufacturing in India. Our products combine innovation, ergonomics, and quality to provide the perfect solution for modern campus living.

Our Products

  • Hostel Furniture: Durable and ergonomic furniture designed for hostel living.
  • Study Tables: Optimized for student productivity and space-saving.
  • Dining & Kitchen Furniture: Modern designs for shared dining spaces.
  • Modular Steel Storage: Highly durable, customizable storage solutions.
  • Metal Beds: Sturdy, comfortable, and long-lasting beds.
  • Cafeteria Furniture: Stylish and robust furniture for common spaces.

Advantages

  • Eco-friendly and recyclable materials.
  • Fire-resistant and non-corrosive properties.
  • Customizable designs with a 15-year warranty.
  • Perfect for humid climates and resistant to pests.
